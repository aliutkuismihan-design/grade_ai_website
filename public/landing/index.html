Good to know

Questions, answered.

Everything teachers ask before their first class set.

How accurate is the AI grading?

Grade AI scores 95%+ on high-confidence predictions. Anything it's unsure about is flagged with a confidence score so you can review it in seconds — you stay in control of the final grade.

Which exam systems and languages does it support?

It knows YKS, A-Level, DELF, IGCSE, EOS, and SAT, grading against the official curriculum rather than a single model answer. Feedback comes in English, French, Turkish, or Spanish.

Can it really read handwriting?

Yes. The handwriting engine reads math symbols, diagrams, and cursive across all four languages — not just printed text.

Is my students' data private?

Built for GDPR from the ground up, with end-to-end encryption in transit and at rest. Student data is never sold or shared. Ever.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. Scan offline in the classroom and grades sync automatically the moment you reconnect.
